The United States has a violent history of opposition and discrimination towards immigrants, both documented and undocumented. Despite being unique individuals with lives, families, and humanity, U.S. Government legislation, xenophobic groups and sentiments, and harmful stereotypes in American pop culture have all contributed to creating a hostile environment where immigrants are perceived as a threat to the country.
